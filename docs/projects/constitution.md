# Kokoro Constitution

Repository: [https://github.com/happykokoro/kokoro-constitution](https://github.com/happykokoro/kokoro-constitution)

Kokoro Constitution is the engineering governance framework for Kokoro Tech. It defines the standards, processes, and rules that apply to all engineering work across the organization.

## Purpose

Engineering teams accumulate informal norms over time. The constitution externalizes those norms into a document that can be referenced, debated, and amended. This makes governance explicit rather than implicit.

The framework covers areas such as:

- Engineering standards and conventions
- Development processes
- Review and audit requirements
- Rules for how the framework itself can be changed

## Relationship to other projects

The Kokoro Claude Skills project implements automation for some of the audit and review processes defined here. The constitution is the policy layer; the skills are part of the tooling layer.

## Status

Under development. The framework is not finalized. Content and scope are subject to change.

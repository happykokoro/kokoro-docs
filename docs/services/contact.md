## What to Include in Your Inquiry

A clear first message saves several back-and-forth exchanges. Include:

- **Project scope** — What are you building? What problem does it solve?
- **Timeline** — When do you need it? Is there a hard deadline?
- **Budget range** — A range is fine. This helps us scope appropriately from the start.
- **Technical requirements** — Languages, frameworks, or platforms you're already using (or open to)
- **Team context** — Will your team maintain the system after delivery? What's their technical background?

## Different Paths for Different Audiences

**Evaluating our services?**
Describe your project, timeline, and budget. We'll assess fit and respond with either a proposal or honest feedback that we're not the right match.

**Interested in investing or partnering?**
Tell us about your fund or organization and what aspect of the platform interests you most. We're building at the intersection of quantitative trading, DeFi, and AI-augmented development — if that aligns with your thesis, let's talk.

**Want to join the team?**
Send your GitHub profile and a note about what excites you. Tell us which part of the stack interests you most (Rust systems, quant finance, DeFi, AI tooling) and what you've built. Read our [culture and values](../philosophy/culture.md) first.
